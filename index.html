About Cleanvo

At Cleanvo Essentials, we believe that a clean home is the foundation of a happy, healthy, and peaceful life. Our journey began with a simple but powerful realization: keeping your home spotless shouldn’t require harsh, toxic chemicals that worry you, nor should premium, highly effective hygiene products be out of reach.

Born in the heart of Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, Cleanvo was founded to bridge this gap. We set out to create a line of home cleaning essentials that deliver commercial-grade, uncompromising strength while remaining completely safe for everyday family life.

🎯 Our Mission

"To replace household germs with absolute peace of mind through safe, powerful, and honest cleaning essentials."

👁️ Our Vision

"To make healthy, toxic-conscious living the effortless choice for every home, everyday."
